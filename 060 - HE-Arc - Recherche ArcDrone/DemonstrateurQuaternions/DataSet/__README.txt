Description des datasets:

Capteur: EM7180
Datasheet: http://www.pnicorp.com/wp-content/uploads/Sentral-Technical-Datasheet-R07.pdf
Frequence de mesure: 100HZ

Les colonnes des datasets représentent (même ordre que datasheet page 25):
	1) Normalized Quaternion – X
	2) Normalized Quaternion – Y
	3) Normalized Quaternion – Z
	4) Normalized Quaternion – W
	5) Quaternion Data Timestamp
	6) Magnetic Field – X Axis
	7) Magnetic Field – Y Axis
	8) Magnetic Field – Z Axis
	9) Magnetometer Interrupt Timestamp
	10) Linear Acceleration – X
	11) Linear Acceleration – Y
	12) Linear Acceleration – Z
	13) Accelerometer Interrupt Timestamp
	14) Rotational Velocity – X
	15) Rotational Velocity – Y
	16) Rotational Velocity – Z
	17) Gyroscope Interrupt Timestamp
